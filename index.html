What We Fix

Common Water Dispenser Problems We Solve

Not Cooling

No cold water usually points to low gas, a tired compressor, thermostat, cooling fan or condenser. We diagnose and fix the exact part.

Not Heating

When the hot tap goes cold, it is often the heating element, thermostat or safety switch. We restore hot water safely.

Leaking Water

Leaks from the tap, bottle holder, pipe, tank or drain tray are traced to the source and sealed so they don't return.

Strange Noise

Loud humming or vibration can mean compressor stress, loose fittings, a fan fault or gas-pressure issues. We quieten it down.

Slow or No Water

Slow flow or no water often comes from blocked pipes, a damaged tap, airlock, pump fault or internal dirt. We clean or replace as needed.

Bad Taste or Smell

Off taste or odour usually means buildup, old filters or scale. Our cleaning, sanitizing and filter service freshens it up.

Tap Replacement

Broken or loose taps are common. We replace hot, cold and normal taps to suit your exact dispenser model.

Gas Refilling

Weak cooling from low gas or a leak? We inspect the cooling line and refill refrigerant where it's genuinely required.
