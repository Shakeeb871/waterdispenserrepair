Geepas Water Dispenser Problems We Fix

Whatever your Geepas unit is doing wrong, we fix it the same day. Tell us the model and the fault when you call, and our technician arrives with the right Geepas parts to test and repair on the spot. Below are the faults we handle most, and how we put each one right.

Geepas Dispenser Not Cooling

When cold water turns warm, the cause is usually low refrigerant gas in the R134a system, a failed compressor, or a dusty condenser. We test the compressor, gas, and cooling system, then recharge or repair it so cold water returns.

Geepas Dispenser Not Heating

No hot water usually means a burnt-out heating element, a tripped dry-heating protection, or scale buildup from Dubai’s hard water. We test the element and thermostat, descale the tank, and confirm the water reaches full temperature.

Digital Touch Panel Not Responding

On digital models such as the GWD17043 and the GWD17041 water bar, the touch display can stop working or the preset temperatures stop holding. We diagnose the control board and restore the panel.

Child Lock or Controls Stuck

Geepas units have a child safety lock that can stay engaged and block hot water. We diagnose the control fault and restore normal operation on each tap.

Geepas Dispenser Leaking

A leak usually starts at the stainless steel tank, a worn seal, or a loose tap fitting. We trace it to the exact source, replace the part, and run the unit to make sure it stays dry.

Noisy Operation

Geepas units use a low-noise compressor, so a sudden loud hum or rattle points to a fault, often a dusty coil or a loose fan. We find the source and quieten the unit down.

Not Dispensing Water

A blocked tap, scale in the tubing, or a stuck push-tap stops the flow. We clear the blockage or replace the tap so water dispenses normally again.

Bad Taste or Smell

Stale or off-tasting water means buildup inside the tank and pipe. We carry out a full clean and sanitization so the water tastes fresh and safe.
