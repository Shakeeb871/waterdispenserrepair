Common Issues

Problems We Solve Every Day

Recognise any of these? We can fix them — usually on the first visit.

🚫 Water Not Cooling

Often caused by low refrigerant gas, a faulty compressor, or a blocked condenser. We diagnose and restore proper cooling.

🌡️ Hot Water Not Heating

Usually a failed heating element or thermostat. We test and replace the faulty component quickly.

💦 Continuous Leaking

Worn seals, cracked tanks or loose fittings cause leaks. We locate the source and seal it for good.

🔊 Loud Noise / Vibration

Noisy operation points to compressor or fan issues. We service the internals to restore quiet running.

⚡ Not Turning On

Power, switch or wiring faults stop your unit working. We safely diagnose and repair electrical problems.

👃 Bad Taste or Smell

Buildup inside the tanks affects taste. Our deep-clean & sanitize service makes your water fresh again.
