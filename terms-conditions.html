About Us

Water Dispenser Repair is a UAE-based home appliance repair company. We repair water dispensers of all major brands and models, including top-load, bottom-load, countertop, and plumbed-in units, for homes, offices, and businesses across the Emirates. Our technicians are certified and carry out repairs on site at your location. You can reach us on +971 50 159 0802 or at admin@waterdispenserrepair.com.

The Services We Provide

We diagnose and repair faults in water dispensers, including cooling and heating problems, leaks, pump and tap faults, sensor and control issues, gas refilling, and full cleaning and sanitization. We aim to offer same-day or next-day service across our coverage areas, depending on availability and your location. We carry common spare parts so that most repairs can be completed in a single visit. Some repairs, however, may require a specific part to be ordered, in which case we will tell you the expected cost and timing before we proceed and arrange a return visit at a time that suits you.
