Aftron Water Dispenser Problems We Fix

Aftron dispensers are built with hot and cold systems, compressor cooling, stainless steel tanks, bi-metal temperature control, LED indicators, and safety protection. We check the failed function, confirm the cause, and repair only the part that needs service.

Aftron Not Cooling

We test the compressor load, cooling coil condition, refrigerant pressure, fan movement, and electrical supply. If the cooling circuit is weak, we restore the cooling path, service the compressor side, or correct the gas-related issue after confirming safe operation.

Aftron Not Heating

We check the heater element, hot tank, wiring path, safety protection, and heater control. Aftron units include protection that helps prevent total drainage from damaging the heater, but the element can still fail, so we replace or repair the affected heating section after testing.

Bi-Metal Temperature Control Fault

We inspect the bi-metal thermostat response, temperature cut-off point, and connection stability. If the control is not regulating correctly, we recalibrate the connection where possible or replace the failed control part with the correct matching type.

Low-Water Safety Cut-Off Stuck

We check the low-water safety mechanism, sensor response, internal contact points, and related wiring. Aftron’s double safety protection needs correct signalling, so we clean, reset, or replace the failed safety section after confirming water-level response.

Leaking From Tank, Seal, or Valve

We inspect the tank seating, valve body, tap base, silicone seal, drain cap, and internal pipe joints. After finding the leak point, we reseal, tighten, replace, or align the affected section to stop water escape safely.

Weak or No Water Flow

We check the tap passage, switch action, tubing, tank outlet, and blockage points. If flow is restricted, we clear the line, correct the tap movement, or replace the affected flow part after checking both hot and cold sides.

Tap or Switch Not Working

We test the tap lever, push switch, button contact, spring movement, and dispenser response. If the switch or tap assembly is worn, loose, or jammed, we repair the contact point or install the correct replacement.

Noisy Operation or Bad Taste

We inspect the compressor seating, fan area, cabinet vibration, tank cleanliness, and water-contact surfaces. We correct loose vibration points and clean or sanitize the dispenser section that affects taste and hygiene.
