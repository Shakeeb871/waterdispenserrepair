Why Is Your Water Dispenser Not Cooling? Causes & Fixes

Home / Blog / Article

Published June 1, 2026 · Water Dispenser Repair Dubai

A water dispenser that no longer delivers cold water is one of the most common problems we see in Dubai homes and offices. The good news: it is usually fixable the same day.

Low refrigerant gas

The most frequent cause is low refrigerant. Over time small leaks reduce the gas level, so the cooling tank can no longer chill the water. A technician will check for leaks, repair them and refill the gas to the correct level.

A faulty compressor

The compressor is the heart of the cooling system. If it is failing or not starting, you will often hear unusual humming or clicking. In many cases it can be repaired or its relay replaced rather than swapping the whole unit.

Blocked airflow or a dirty condenser

Dust on the condenser coils at the back traps heat and reduces cooling. A simple clean can restore performance — and it is included in our service visits.

Thermostat problems

A faulty thermostat may stop the cooling cycle from running. Replacing this inexpensive part often brings the cold water straight back.
